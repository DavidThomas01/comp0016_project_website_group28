GDPR & Legal Compliance

This section documents data privacy considerations and GDPR [2], [3] compliance measures implemented in the platform.

Data minimisation

The platform collects only the minimum data necessary for testing and does not store personal information.

Local-first storage

All test data is stored locally on the user's device with no transmission to external servers.

Right to erasure

Users can delete all stored test data and configurations at any time through the platform interface.

External models

The platform integrates with external AI models; users are responsible for ensuring compliance with regulations defined by the respective providers.

Chatbot provider

The platform's chatbot uses an OpenAI model; all interactions are subject to the provider's terms and conditions.
