# Future work (backlog)

Ordered **most important → least** within each category. Not part of coursework delivery; for Avanade / follow-on.

---

## 1. Learning, testing & demos

How people explore the product, run comparisons, and use it without live APIs.

| Priority | Idea | One-line |
|----------|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Compare two mitigation stacks** | Same prompt, two mitigation setups—risk scores and full replies side by side. |
| 2 | **Guided learning paths** | Pre-built routes (“~30 min” vs “deeper”) so new users are not lost in the full catalogue. |
| 3 | **Prompt autopsy** | After a run, highlight text that likely drove the score and suggest rewrites—explain the number. |
| 4 | **Model matchup** | One test prompt to two providers/models; compare refusals, leaks, tone. |
| 5 | **Read-only demo mode** | Fixed scenarios and canned answers so demos work without API keys (schools, meetups, locked-down networks). |
| 6 | **Exportable lesson packs** | Bundle vulnerabilities, mitigations, suggested tests into ZIP or printable outline for repeatable sessions. |

## 2. Community, content & operations

How the knowledge base scales, stays trustworthy, and is edited without engineering.

| Priority | Idea | One-line |
|----------|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Contributor checklist** | Fields, example prompt, risk labels—consistent community additions. |
| 2 | **Community knowledge base** | Curated community write-ups next to official content (moderated). |
| 3 | **Content management system (CMS)** | Authors update pages in a writing UI instead of the codebase. |
| 4 | **Real incident footnotes** | Short, concrete notes per vulnerability (“this pattern often appears when…”). |
| 5 | **Federated knowledge** | Optional sharing of anonymised mitigations/notes into a shared index. |
| 6 | **Community model directory** | Models with practical notes, community votes, pass/fail anecdotes—not only marketing specs. |
